Innovation

SAP

Connect

A B2B web portal for your customers to order online and track the order status.​

Engage

Our client was using SAP for business already, so we created a technical user account in SAP RFC communication & used SAP RFC​ system to sync data between SAP and our​ web portal.​

Transform

Products, discounts, packaging details, delivery details are obtained from the SAP system and presented in our web portal. The customer can order and track the order status in the portal.The orders from customers and all the transactions from the web portal are also captured in the SAP system without disturbing the existing SAP workflow.​

Manufacturing

Connect

The meters in your factories or campuses can be monitored without any hassles.​

Engage

Our industrial IoT application can monitor the smart meter installed in your factories. The IoT application collects metrics from every smart meter for every second and helps you to identify and detect abnormal activities that occurs in any of the smart meters.​

Transform

You can view all the smart meters status using a visualization tool and generate reports.​ With these reports, you can analyze the usage and efficiency of your hi-tech machines in the factories.​

Farming

Connect

A venture to facilitate farmers connect with the veterinarians so they could get medical assistance for their cattle using WhatsApp.​

Engage

A digital ethnoveterinary chatbot designed to support direct conversation in regional languages with the medical expert team.​ An audio prescription along with timely reminders for medicine intakes will be sent to the farmers once their cattles are diagnosed.​

Transform

Farmers can use YourFarm chatbot through WhatsApp to reach out to the experts when seeking medical advice for their cattle.​ Farmers get instant support from experts for any health issues pertaining to their livestock 2 mins (avg.) and hence protecting their livelihood​
